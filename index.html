My Project "Plan my Uni"

Overview

“Plan my Uni” is a mobile device application which will use AI and machine learning technology to build a personalized and unique study plan for a student. The plan will divide the total work in one semester to specific tasks into students’ work planner, by analyzing each student's preference, lifestyle, the difficulty of assignment and other possible factors. Besides, "Plan my Uni" will provide the most related events suggestions inside and outside the university, such as “Orientation Week” for new students, to help students to integrate into their university life. The aim of “Plan my Uni” is to assist students to achieve their academic success through a personalised and organised plan.

Motivation

There has been an increase in the discussion of student success over recent years in the IT area and higher education institutes with every indication that this concern will continue to expand. According to Grajek and the 2018–2019 EDUCAUSE IT Issues Panel (2019), how to apply new technology, such as AI, to help students achieve success is in the list of “2019 top 10 IT issues”, and many universities are trying to use new technologies to become a Student-Centred Institution. Moreover, as a university student and a student mentor, there are some ridiculous but common, chaotic planning problems that happen in a university student’s life. For instance, an anxious mentee shows up in the SLAM’s room with an assignment that is due on that day, but he had to do other assignments first. Therefore, “Plan my Uni” will help universities assist students because it is an ideal study tool to plan work more efficiently.

Discription

The first and the most critical feature of "Plan my Uni" is generating a suitable and personalized plan for the student. "Plan my Uni" is not a simple plain timetable that schedules manually by the student; in contrast, the majority of study schedule will be done by AI system. “Plan my Uni” will have a user test to collect information from the user, and access the student, assignments and all relevant data from university to analysis their study behavior and preferences after getting permission from students and institute (safety policy and corporation policy from universities). All the collected information enable the system to divide and allocate different works into a personalized and well-organized routine timetable to help the student to follow their assignment and ease their workload when it is close to due date.

The second feature is the events suggestions system. "Plan my Uni" is not only just a timetable for study but also aim to help students to participate actively in their university life. Participation of society is another critical factor to student success rather than just academic success; additionally, events, such as volunteering and clubs' activities, will help students to learn valuable experience before their graduating. Therefore, the event suggestion system will show a variety of related activities based on the student’s information and put the selected event into their plan.

In order to motivate students to follow the plan, "Plan my Uni" has a motivation feature. This feature contains student rating system and awards system. To be more specific, the rating system is slimmer to the level-up system of some RPG game. There are a bronze, silver, gold, diamond title, and each title have ten levels. For example, every new user will have a title of identities, such as L1 bronze, and an experience bar. When students finish their work on time, they will gain some experiences into an experience bar. When experience bar is full, students will level up and gain a new title, such as L2 bronze. For rewards system, if students gain a new title, he will also have some rewards. There are plenty of kinds of rewards, such as discounts coupon of retail stores which university students need.

Moreover, rewards can be stored as credits, and it is called “unicoin”. Students can use “unicoin” to purchase small gifts from pop-up gifts store in the university. With this feature, “Plan my Uni” can make student fell interesting and get motivated while they study to ensure students to follow the plan and motivate them as best as it can.

Another exciting feature of "Plan my Uni" is connecting students based on their similar or complementary information. There are huge differences between universities and high schools. Hoyt (2018) stated that in high school, most students know everyone in the class, but a student is lucky to know one person in the university class. Because of extensive collected data of students, “Plan my Uni” will provide users a friend list based on the data. For example, An IT student can view the suggestion list of other students who have the same lectures or tutorials. This feature will connect students together, and increase their relationship.

Tools and Technologies

Firstly, as a mobile application, “Plan my Uni” needs a programming software to write it. Python should be a suitable programming tool to do this task. Secondly, “Plan my Uni” also require several infrastructures of artificial intelligence to run it. At this stage, Python is the programming software to write the code. However, the most important thing is data and data storage. "Plan my Uni" will use the data from the database center that universities are currently used, and have own database hardware to support the application.

Skills Required

“Plan my Uni” is a project that requires high-level programming skills to make it; therefore, it needs a team or even more. People who work the project should master python and all other possible computing skills. Moreover, a database manager is essential because the project also requires data management. Finding people with these skills can be a challenge, but it is possible since the project will be provided to governments departures or high-education institutions. These organizations will have enough human resources and funds to accomplish this project.

Outcome

If "Plan my Uni" is successful, it will be huge progress for universities to become a student-centered institution. With “Plan my Uni”, students will have a plan that is designed by their data. They will find that their lifes become more relaxed because they always have an organized and scientific plan. Moreover, “Plan my Uni" not only can help students to achieve their academic success but also help them to build a friendship with others and participate in their university events. In general, "Plan my Uni" will be a worthy project to bring high-education to the future.
